Occupancy generation vs. UniScene-v2 — λ=0.05

After submission, we found that the released UniScene-v2 code sets the noise-prior weight to λ=0.05 whereas our paper reports λ=0.15. This page re-evaluates UniScene-v2 at λ=0.05. The change is marginal and does not alter any of the paper's contributions or conclusions: ROCC still outperforms UniScene-v2 by a wide margin on every metric and set. We report it for full transparency. Results at the paper's λ=0.15 are here.
